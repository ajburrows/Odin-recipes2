Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage (optional)
  • 28 ounces canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • For the Cheese Mixture:
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Additional Ingredients:
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for topping

Cheese Mixture

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Additional Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions

  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for topping

instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Make the Meat Sauce:
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until soft.
  4. Add the ground beef and sausage, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, basil, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce for about 20-30 minutes, letting the flavors meld together.
  7. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
  8. In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  9. Assemble the Lasagna:
  10. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  11. Layer 3 cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  12. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, and sprinkle with one cup of mozzarella cheese.
  13. Repeat the layers twice more, ending with a layer of noodles topped with meat sauce and additional mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  14. Bake:
  15. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  16. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden.
  17. Rest and Serve:
  18. Let the lasagna sit for about 15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set and makes it easier to serve.
